  • Your name (December 14, 2016)

    •

    "Your Name (Kimi no Na wa)" is a critically acclaimed anime film directed by Makoto Shinkai. It tells
    the story of two teenagers, Mitsuha and Taki, who find themselves mysteriously swapping bodies and
    lives. As they navigate the challenges of this strange connection, they develop a profound bond that
    transcends time and space.

    The film is renowned for its stunning animation, emotional depth, and a compelling narrative that
    blends fantasy with a heartfelt exploration of love and fate. Its striking visuals and evocative
    soundtrack by Radwimps contribute to an immersive experience that has resonated with audiences
    worldwide.
